What is Redakt?

Redakt is a simple encryption/decryption tool that offers a way to protect any text you publish on the Internet (posts, messages, comments). Redakt protects your content from being easily readable by bots, scrappers, algorithms or AI.

Redakt is an open source project launched in 2023
